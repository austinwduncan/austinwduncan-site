## About This Series

The local church is God's primary instrument for making disciples and displaying the gospel to the world. Yet many Christians have a thin and functional view of the church — it is a place you go, a service you attend, a community you consume.

This series recovers a thicker, more biblical vision: the church as the body and bride of Christ, the pillar and buttress of truth, the new covenant community gathered around Word and sacrament.

## Sessions

### Session 1: The Church in God's Plan
From Israel to the church — the continuity and discontinuity of redemptive history. The church was not Plan B.

### Session 2: The Marks of a True Church
Word rightly preached. Sacraments rightly administered. Church discipline rightly practiced. Why these three things constitute the church.

### Session 3: Baptism and the Lord's Supper
What the ordinances are, what they signify, and why they matter for the life of the congregation.

### Session 4: Church Membership
Membership is not a cultural leftover — it is a covenantal commitment. What we owe one another.

### Session 5: Church Discipline
The most misunderstood and neglected practice in evangelical churches. Why it is an act of love.

### Session 6: The Mission of the Church
The Great Commission, the local church, and the global spread of the gospel. What the church is sent to do.
