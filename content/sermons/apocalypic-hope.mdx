## 3 MAIN GROUPS IN BIBLE PROPHECY

There are three main groups to keep in mind when reading bible prophecy/apocalyptic writing. 

1. The Gentile Nations
2. The Jewish Nation of Israel
3. [The Church](/sermons/the-church-of-the-open-door/8/30/2021)

Now, the reason that I bring this up as we get into the [tribulation again today](/sermons/triumph-and-tribulation/02/04/2024), because we want to keep each one of those groups and God’s purpose, plan, and design (prophetically) for those individual groups. So, it’s important to keep them in their proper place and proper category. Now, with that being said we’ll be looking at two different focuses before we get to our main text – just to sort of set us up mentally for today.
